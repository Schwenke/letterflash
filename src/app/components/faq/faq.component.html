Goal

You get six chances to guess a secret word

Perfect clues are in the secret word and in the correct position

Partial clues are in the secret word but in the wrong position

FAQ

What is hard mode?

When enabled, guesses must include all previous clues

What is extreme mode?

When enabled, the secret word will pull from the entire dictionary instead of a curated list. Incredibly difficult!

How do I share a word?

When you finish a game, click "Share" to copy the results into your clipboard. This includes a special link. When someone clicks the link, the game will launch using the word you shared!
