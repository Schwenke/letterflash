Goal

You get six chances to guess a secret word. Make a guess by typing in a valid word and pressing enter.

Perfect clues are in the secret word and in the correct position.

Partial clues are in the secret word but in the wrong position.

FAQ

What is hard mode?

When enabled, guesses must include all previous clues.

What is extreme mode?

When enabled, the secret word will pull from the entire dictionary instead of a curated list. Incredibly difficult!

How do I share a puzzle?

When you finish a game, click the "Share a link to this puzzle" button to copy a link to your clipboard.

Then, share the link with your friends. They will be given the secret word you just solved as their puzzle!

Copying the results from a share link will hide the secret word and include a link to that puzzle.
